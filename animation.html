Animation and Game Design

Illustraton takes on a life of its own when you add the third and forth dimensions – depth and time. Creating an image which dances across the screen has delighted and inspired all of us for decades. With the advent of powerful desktop computers, refined rendering methods and affordable software, creative people are finally seeing their dreams take a breath – and live.

Berkeley City College’s multimedia faculty and creators have developed a curriculum which provides a well-rounded education in a fast-paced and relevant learning environment.
